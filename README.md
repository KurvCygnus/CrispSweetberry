## Project Overview

> **Crisp Sweetberry** is a **vanilla-focused QoL Minecraft mod** that aims to enhance and expand gameplay in exploration, combat, automation, and building —
  **with minimal stylistic intrusion**.

Crisp Sweetberry is designed to be highly compatible with most vanilla-friendly mods.\
However, combining CSB with mods that heavily alter **core mechanics or progression systems** may lead to unexpected behavior.

*This mod is currently under active development.*

---

## Design Philosophy

Crisp Sweetberry strives to be:

* **Vanilla-friendly**
* **Mechanically intuitive**
* **Discoverable through natural gameplay**

Crisp Sweetberry intentionally avoids:

* Replacing vanilla systems with entirely new progression frameworks
* Large-scale world generation or biome overhauls
* Forcing players to read external wikis to understand **basic mechanics**

---

## Feature Preview

Crisp Sweetberry primarily focuses on:

* Quality-of-life improvements for exploration and survival
* Utility items that integrate naturally with vanilla mechanics
* Subtle extensions to combat and progression
* Optional systems that **reward curiosity rather than enforce learning**

## Mod Information

Crisp Sweetberry is currently available **only** for:

* **Minecraft 26.1**
* **NeoForge Mod Loader**

Major updates will be released in **themed waves**, similar to official Minecraft updates.\
If certain extensible features become popular enough, they may be split into standalone library mods.

## Installation

1. Download **NeoForge** for **Minecraft 26.1**.
   You can find detailed installation instructions in NeoForge’s official documentation:
   at [this link](https://docs.neoforged.net/user/docs/)

2. Download **Crisp Sweetberry** from our release page, CurseForge, or Modrinth.

3. Place the mod file into the **`mods`** folder of your NeoForge Minecraft installation.

4. **You’re ready to rock! Enjoy your journey with Crisp Sweetberry.**

## Contributing

Crisp Sweetberry is still in an early development stage.\
Before the first stable release, we do **not** accept any contributions.

However, we **welcome**:

* Design discussions
* Suggestions
* Conceptual feedback

### Contribution Principles

* Crisp Sweetberry is a project that prioritizes **design philosophy and maintainability**
* We do **not** pursue speed or feature stacking
* **Fabric support will not be considered before late-stage content stabilization**
  *(If you are experienced and interested, you may fork the project and proceed independently.)*
* All merged code must be:

  * Easy to understand
  * Easy to maintain
  * Friendly to modding beginners

We also welcome contributions in:

* **Art**
* **Localization / translation**

Every form of contribution is respected.

If you agree with these principles, you are already a great fit for Crisp Sweetberry — regardless of experience level.\
If you have any interest in joining us, you can go further to our [contribution guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md).
